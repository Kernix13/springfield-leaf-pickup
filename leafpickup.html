Below is the current year leaf collection schedule. Every year 2 precincts are rotated from the bottom of the schedule to the top of the schedule. Rotating the schedule each year ensures that no precincts are always the first or last each year.

*Precinct = Zone + Ward

The numbers below are a combination of the Zone number and Ward number. For example, Precinct 4-1 is Zone 4 and Ward 1, or 4-1.

    Assumptions made in creating this schedule

    My name is Jim Kernicky and I have a web design business. I also live in Springfield and created this schedule app for myself. I contacted the township via email but never heard back from them on some of my questions. Here are the assumptions I had to make to create this schedule:

    • The 1st week of leaf pickup is the week of the first Monday in November (this is incorrect).
    • The # of precints do not increase or decrease.
    • The order that precincts follow in the schedule is constant and does not change.
    • The # of precincts per week does not change: 3 precincts for the two holiday weeks, 4 precincts for the other weeks.
    • The # of weeks of leaf pickup (currently 6) does not change.

    Contact me if you know that 1 or more of those assumptions are wrong.
